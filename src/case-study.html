ILES HR

Case Study

Introduction

With more than 10 years of experience, ILES Consulting provides consultancy services to its customers aiming for a better life, in evaluating opportunities in different countries.

Problem Statement

Their old website were cluttered and had no space to neatly place the information they wanted to give their customers. Customers were leaving the site confused, without getting the information they needed.

What We Did

We gathered nearly 20 sub-titles under 4 main titles and gave each title one of the colors of the company, allowing customers to reach where they need them faster.

Target Audience

As the main target; people who aim to work and live abroad, especially in the UK.

Tools We Used For This Case

Adobe Xd

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe After Effects

Webflow

WordPress

We designed a Lottie Animation in Adobe After Effects for Landing Page about 4 main titles instead of complicated designs to create a better visual for the people who just came to the website.

We gathered nearly 20 sub-titles under 4 main titles and gave each title one of the colors of the company, allowing customers to reach where they need them faster.

We put a list of every main titles into their page so when a customer comes to learn about any sub-title, they can see any other similar topic from the sticky list at the left of the page.

We build applications the way it should be.
